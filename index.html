Guy Harris has been copywriting since before he knew what copywriting was. He was also twerking and planking before he knew what they were, however he's since realised he can't make a living doing either of those things full-time.

He came 11th at AWARD School in 2016 and will not be stopped until he's given the opportunity to take the advertising world by storm.
